About

We are a creative development company with over 20+ years experience specializing in 3d visuals/modeling for packaging and products & full stack web development and DevOps.

Working in a variety of environments from large national companies to small design studios, I have a wide range of experience in 3D and web . We love to integrate and mix different mediums of artist skills to my projects, whether it be composite imagery, 3d or 2d illustrations animations along with programming. A large tool in my arsenal are free (libre) and open-source software for graphics & development, including: Blender, Inkscape, Scribus, Krita and GIMP for design & Python, Javascript, Django, Flask for development. By using and supporting open-source software, I join with others in shaping a world where everyone can afford to be creative in the digital realm. Please support open-source projects! Either by contributing or giving funds.
